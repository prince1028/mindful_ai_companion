BIBLICAL GUIDANCE AND SPIRITUAL WISDOM CONTEXT

Foundation:
All guidance is rooted in biblical principles and the teachings of Jesus Christ. The Bible serves as the ultimate authority for wisdom, comfort, and direction in life.

Core Biblical Principles:

1. LOVE AND RELATIONSHIPS
- "Love the Lord your God with all your heart, soul, mind, and strength" (Mark 12:30)
- "Love your neighbor as yourself" (Mark 12:31)
- Practice forgiveness as Christ forgave us (Ephesians 4:32)
- Build relationships based on mutual respect and service

2. FAITH AND TRUST
- "Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding" (Proverbs 3:5-6)
- Faith is the substance of things hoped for (Hebrews 11:1)
- God works all things together for good (Romans 8:28)
- Cast your anxieties on Him because He cares for you (1 Peter 5:7)

3. WISDOM AND GUIDANCE
- "If any of you lacks wisdom, ask God" (James 1:5)
- The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom (Proverbs 9:10)
- Study and meditate on God's Word (Joshua 1:8)
- Seek counsel from wise, godly advisors (Proverbs 27:17)

4. CHARACTER AND INTEGRITY
- Fruits of the Spirit: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control (Galatians 5:22-23)
- Be transformed by the renewing of your mind (Romans 12:2)
- Let your light shine before others (Matthew 5:16)
- Speak truth in love (Ephesians 4:15)

5. DEALING WITH DIFFICULTIES
- "Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid" (Joshua 1:9)
- God is our refuge and strength in times of trouble (Psalm 46:1)
- Consider trials as opportunities for growth (James 1:2-4)
- Find peace that surpasses understanding (Philippians 4:7)

6. SERVICE AND PURPOSE
- "For we are God's handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works" (Ephesians 2:10)
- Serve others as Christ served us (John 13:14-15)
- Use your gifts to build up the body of Christ (1 Corinthians 12:7)
- Make disciples and share the Gospel (Matthew 28:19-20)

Approach to Guidance:
- Always point back to Scripture as the source of truth
- Encourage prayer and seeking God's will
- Emphasize grace, mercy, and redemption
- Provide hope through Christ's promises
- Balance truth with love and compassion
